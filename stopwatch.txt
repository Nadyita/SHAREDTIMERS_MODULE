To start the stopwatch:
<highlight><tab><symbol>stopwatch start<end>

To stop the stopwatch and show the time elapsed:
<highlight><tab><symbol>stopwatch stop<end>

To add a lap with an optional name to the stopwatch:
<highlight><tab><symbol>stopwatch lap<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>stopwatch lap 'my name'<end>

To view the current time elapsed without stopping the stopwatch:
<highlight><tab><symbol>stopwatch view<end>
